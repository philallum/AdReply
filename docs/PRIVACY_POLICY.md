# AdReply Privacy Policy

## Data Collection
AdReply does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers.

## Local Storage
The extension stores the following data locally on your device:
- Template preferences and settings
- Usage statistics for template rotation
- User-selected business category preferences
- Promotional URL settings

## Data Usage
All data processing occurs locally within your browser. No information is sent to external servers or third parties.

## Facebook Integration
The extension only reads publicly available content on Facebook to provide contextual template suggestions. It does not access private messages, personal information, or account details.

## Third-Party Services
AdReply does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

## Data Security
All user data is stored securely using Chrome's built-in storage APIs with appropriate encryption and access controls.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in extension updates and communicated to users.

## Contact
For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact the development team.
